[[vmatrix]]
== vmatrix

=== LaTeX

[source,latex]
----
\begin{vmatrix}x \\ y \\ z\end{vmatrix}
----


=== UnicodeMath

[source,unicodemath]
----
⒱(x&y&z)
----

----
